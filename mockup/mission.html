Mission

The mission of the Cultural Fair is:
To increase awareness and understanding of, and pride in, the cultural and ethnic diversity of the community and its international ties
To create an atmosphere of neighborly celebration
To make the community aware of the international connections of its individuals, businesses and organizations
To help bring ethnic minorities into the community mainstream

The Cultural Fair started in 2004 and has been held annually since then. Each year over 1,600 people attend the Fair. A village celebration is created with people of all ages and many cultures participating in sharing stories, music, performances, and food.
